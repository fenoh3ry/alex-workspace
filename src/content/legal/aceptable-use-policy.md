This Acceptable Use Policy outlines rules for using our services, APIs, and platform features. By using our product, you agree to abide by the practices below.

## 1. Purpose

Our platform is intended to help developers build, test, and deploy modern applications. All usage must be lawful, respectful, and technically appropriate.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

You may **not** use our services to:

### 2.1 Harm or Exploit Others

- Harass, stalk, or threaten individuals
- Exploit minors or promote illegal activities
- Disseminate hate speech, abusive content, or violence

### 2.2 Disrupt or Abuse the Platform

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to any system
- Launch denial-of-service (DoS) attacks
- Interfere with or degrade the performance of our services

### 2.3 Violate Intellectual Property

- Upload or share content you don't have the rights to
- Infringe trademarks, copyrights, or patents

### 2.4 Misuse APIs or Dashboards

- Circumvent API rate limits or quotas
- Use bots or scripts to overload endpoints
- Automate abusive behavior or data scraping

## 3. Security and Integrity

You are responsible for:

- Keeping API keys and credentials secure
- Reporting vulnerabilities or abuse responsibly
- Notifying us immediately of security breaches

## 4. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warning or account suspension
- Revocation of access to our services
- Legal action in severe cases

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time.
