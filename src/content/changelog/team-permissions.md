Our latest release brings advanced **team management and access control** to your projects. Whether you’re collaborating with a small internal team or managing multiple client environments, you now have full control over who can do what.

## What's New

### Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Assign roles like:

- **Admin** – Full access to everything including billing, integrations, and team settings.
- **Editor** – Can manage content, data, and deploy updates.
- **Viewer** – Read-only access to project data and dashboards.

Roles can be customized or extended to fit your workflow.

### Per-Project Visibility

Keep your workspaces organized and secure:

- Invite users to specific projects only
- Prevent cross-project data access
- Limit visibility of internal tools and experiments

### Audit Logs (Coming Soon)

Track every action taken within your organization, from content edits to permission changes, with full timestamped logs.

## Use Cases

- Set up an internal team where only developers can deploy.
- Invite clients with view-only access to review analytics.
- Restrict new contributors to a staging project for testing.

Granular permissions give you the control you need — without slowing down collaboration. Head to your **Team Settings** to start configuring roles now.
